SHARK TANK ANALYSIS
What's the deal with the sharks!!
Shark Tank is an Emmy winning reality show that features a panel of potential investors, called "sharks," who consider offers from aspiring entrepreneurs seeking investments for their business or product. This analysis provides a view of the closed deals by the sharks and their top choice of category for investment.
The Analysis

Deals in Numbers

It is very evident from the graph that the top categories where deals are presented and closed are Food, Fashion and Lifestyle.

Conversion Rate

The Categories look a little more close when we look at the percentages. The two categories that have similar deal conversion rates are Lifestyle and Fitness.

Whats the Valuation

The average valuation chart provides a view of how the valuation looks like for each of the categories. Media Companies are highly valued companies in show.
